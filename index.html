Portfolio

Navika

Navika is our most developed app and it's main goal is to get users familiarized with the difficult grammar points of the Serbo-Croation family, which includes Croatian, Serbian, Bosnian, and Montenegrin. Users can practice concepts such as declension, verb conjugations, cyrillic, and more.

HanDrills

HanDrills is a spin-off of Navika, and it's aim is the familiarize the user with the most difficults grammar points of the Korean language. Users can practice difficult concepts such as using native and sino korean numbers, verb conjugations, indirect quoting, among others.

Rise and Thrive

Rise and Thrive is a WordPress website that we developed for a client who wanted to create a platform for their clients to read information about their personal training services. The site includes features such as access to a schedule, account creation, store front, PayPal integration for accepting credit card payments, and custom images.

About Casitek

Casitek is a web development company that was founded by Nelson Samayoa in 2020. Casitek operates in a team of two experienced software developers who absolutely love what they do. Combined we have over 16 years in the industry and have worked on a variety of projects, from small websites to large scale web applications. We are currently working on a variety of projects, including a mobile apps for language learning, web applications for a variety of clients, and a web application for a local non-profit organization.

Our pricing model is simple. We charge a flat rate of $100 per hour for all of our services. We are also willing to work on a project for a fixed price, which is determined by the scope of the project. We work as a team on all of our projects, which allows us to be more efficient and to deliver a better product to our clients. We also never outsource our work to other developers, which means that we know every line of code we write
