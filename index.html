Nelson Samayoa

Professional software developer since 2010, graduate of Oregon State University. My original real passion in life is language learning. I don't mean linguistics specifically, although that does of course interest me as well. I mean literally learning languages for fun. To most people it's a chore to be avoided but for me it's how I relax. The feeling of being able to decipher something that only a few hours ago was completely meaningless is a feeling I'm probably addicted to. Fortunately for me, this natural drive helps me a lot as a software developer because ideally every day is a day in which I come to understand something that I did not understand when I woke up that morning.

I have to make an effort keep myself from having too many hobbies because there are so many things that interest me. This page describes some of the main topics that make my life interesting (in addition than family and friends of course).
